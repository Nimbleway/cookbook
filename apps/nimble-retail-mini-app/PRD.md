# Nimble Retail Intelligence Experience

## Vision

Traditional Digital Shelf Analytics platforms provide static reports, scheduled refreshes, and predefined dashboards.

Nimble provides real-time retail intelligence powered by live retailer search data.

This application should demonstrate that difference in under 30 seconds.

The experience should answer:

> What can Nimble tell me about my category right now?

A prospect should leave believing:

* Static reports are outdated
* Nimble provides answers on demand
* Nimble can analyze my category instantly
* I want to see my own business
