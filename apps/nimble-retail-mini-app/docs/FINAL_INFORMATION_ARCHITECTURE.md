# Final Information Architecture Review

**Lens:** a guided category review an exec walks through — not a rack of dashboard modules.

## The core problem
The nav is **9 feature tabs** — *Differences · Earned vs Bought · Takeaways · My Brand · Markets · Ask · Track Over Time · Report · Numbers*. It mixes **analyses** (Differences), **actions** (Ask, Report), and **a data drawer** (Numbers) in one flat list. No narrative — it reads "here are our features," not "here's your category."

## Reframe: navigation = the 5 executive questions
Collapse 9 tabs → **5 narrative steps**, each answering a question a VP actually asks:

| Step (nav label) | Question it answers | What lives here today | Signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **What's happening** | What's happening on my shelf? | Hero answer + Differences + Takeaways | Competition + Difference |
| **Why** | Why does it look this way? | Earned vs Bought | Risk |
| **My brand** | How do I compare? | Run My Brand | Competition |
| **The opening** | Where's the opportunity? | "Up for grabs" + Markets | Opportunity + Difference |
| **What's next** | What should I do? | Ask (persistent) · Track over time · Talk to Nimble | Monitoring + Conversion |

Turns the rail into a **progress spine** through a story, not a menu.

## Section-by-section verdict

| Section | Verdict | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Differences** (cross-retailer) | ⭐ Most valuable — the #1 differentiator | Keep, anchor "What's happening" |
| **Earned vs Bought** | ⭐ Strong, distinct | Keep as "Why" |
| **My Brand** | ⭐ High potential, currently generic | Keep, **personalize** (see AI review) |
| **Track Over Time** | ⭐ Strong conversion bridge | Keep under "What's next" |
| **Takeaways (3 things)** | Valuable as the signal catch-all | **Merge** into "What's happening" |
| **Markets** | Good capability teaser | **Merge** into "The opening" |
| **Ask** | Valuable but mis-placed as a tab | **Action**, not a destination — persistent |
| **Report** | Not an analysis | **A CTA**, not a tab |
| **Numbers** | The dashboard drawer | **Action** ("See the data"), never a headline tab |

## Recommendations (scored)

| # | Recommendation | Exec Impact | Nimble Diff | Conf Value | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Re-label nav to the 5 executive questions | High | Med | High | **Med** |
| 2 | Demote Ask / Report / Numbers from tabs to actions | High | Low | Med | **Low** |
| 3 | Merge Takeaways → "What's happening", Markets → "The opening" | Med | Low | Med | **Low** |
| 4 | "Step n of 5" progress cue so it reads as a guided review | Med | Low | Med | Med |

## Verdicts
**Must change before launch:** #1 (5-question nav) + #2 (Ask/Report/Numbers → actions) — the single biggest "stops feeling like a dashboard" move.
**Nice to have:** #3 merges, #4 progress cue.
**Do not build:** more tabs, a filters panel, a second data view. Every new tab is a step backward.
