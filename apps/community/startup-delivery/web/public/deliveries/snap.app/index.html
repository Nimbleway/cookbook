Positioning brief

Why this wedge is worth testing now

Tuft, Groomsoft, MoeGo, and ShakeYourTail are all B2B grooming management platforms where pet owners book *through* groomer websites—none prioritizes a consumer-first, real-time last-minute availability marketplace where pet owners can search and book same-day slots like Uber or DoorDash. The gap is a direct-to-consumer app that surfaces groomer availability *instantly* and emphasizes speed and urgency, not groomer admin tools.

FAQ

How is Snap different from booking through a groomer's website?

Groomer software like Tuft and Groomsoft are built for shops to manage their own bookings. Snap is a consumer app—you search across multiple groomers at once, see live availability, and book in seconds. No hunting, no calling around.

Can I book for a specific time, or only same-day?

Both. Search for same-day urgent slots or browse availability weeks ahead. The difference: Snap shows you what's actually open *right now*, not a calendar of maybe-available times.

Are these real groomers or a service like DoorDash?

Real, licensed groomers in your area. Snap is a marketplace that connects you directly—they groom your dog at their shop or yours, depending on what they offer.

What if I need a specific groomer or breed specialist?

Filter by groomer rating, breed experience, services (mobile, at-home, shop), and price. Read reviews from other pet owners before booking.
