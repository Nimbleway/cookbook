FAQ

How fast can I actually get my dog groomed?

Depending on your location and groomer availability, many bookings happen within 2–4 hours. Open the app, see available slots, and book instantly.

Do I have to use a chain or big groomer?

No. Groom Now connects you to independent and small-business groomers in your area with real-time open capacity—giving you choice and faster availability.

Is this more expensive than booking in advance?

Same-day grooming may carry a small premium over advance bookings, but you're paying for speed and convenience, not a subscription or retail markup.

What if no one is available right now?

You'll see the next available slots across nearby groomers. You can also set an alert and we'll notify you when a same-day slot opens.
