Positioning brief

Why this wedge is worth testing now

Existing competitors (MoeGo, Tuft, Groomsoft, ShakeYourTail) are B2B platforms built for groomer business management, not pet owner discovery. None offer a consumer-first, demand-side app that helps dog owners find and book last-minute available slots from nearby groomers—they assume owners already know their groomer or browse a groomer's own booking page. This leaves a gap for a mobile-first, push-notification-driven marketplace that surfaces real-time availability to pet owners as the primary experience.

FAQ

How is Pounce different from just calling my groomer?

Pounce shows you *all* available groomers near you in one place, with real-time slots, reviews, and photos—no phone tag. If your regular groomer is booked, you'll see alternatives instantly.

Do I need to set up an account for my dog?

Yes, but it takes 30 seconds. Add your dog's name, breed, and any grooming notes (matted fur, sensitive skin, etc.). Groomers see this before you book, so there are no surprises.

Are the groomers on Pounce vetted?

Yes. Every groomer is verified, insured, and reviewed by pet owners. We only partner with established, professional groomers.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment. Groomers have cancellation policies too, so check when you book.
